The problem

Endometriosis affects 1 in 10 women, yet diagnosis can take years and treatment options remain limited, expensive, and often invasive.

What we're building

Ovella combines TENS therapy, localized heat monitoring, and adaptive algorithms to explore more personalized, continuous support for managing chronic pelvic pain.
